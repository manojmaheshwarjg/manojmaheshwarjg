## About Me

I'm a Product Manager with about 4 years of experience building AI/ML products and full-stack solutions. Currently grinding through my MS in Artificial Intelligence at University at Buffalo while trying to bridge the gap between product thinking and actual code.

I've been lucky enough to ship 7+ products from scratch that collectively generated over $2M in revenue. I founded Hurrae Ventures where we hit $20k in 8 months building MVPs for US startups. These days, I'm learning that understanding the theory is one thing, but implementing it? That's a whole different ball game.

What gets me excited: building AI products that actually solve real problems, shipping fast, and figuring out how to turn product ideas into working code.
